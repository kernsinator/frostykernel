What is FrostyKernel?

This is a place to melt your brain in a linguistic fashion.

The FrostyKernel crew is passionate about three things: puzzles, languages, and web development. When we discovered dozens of linguistics-based puzzles featuring languages from all over the world, we had no choice but to build a website about it.

Wait, so... why exactly does this site exist?

The puzzles featured here were created by brilliant individuals belonging to organizations such as the NACLO and its predecessor The Linguistics Challenge. Some of the original pages haven't been well-maintained, however, and our mission is to preserve the essence of each puzzle while providing functional answer-checking and an accessible design. Our source material is linked and credited throughout, but take a moment to check out the organizations who made this site possible:

Organizations

The Linguistics Challenge

A predecessor to theNACLO whose challenges emphasize cultural awareness and language preservation. Now defunct, but the site is still maintainedhere.

NACLO

The North American Computation Linguistics Open is a problem-solving contest for high-schoolers, but the puzzles are challenging and engaging regardless of age or background. You don't need prior knowledge about linguistics whatsoever to participate — just your critical thinking skills.
