Kurmanji Kurdish ●

Puzzle by N. Bailey

The Kurdish people number at least 25 million. Kurdish is an important member of the Iranian branch of the Indo-European language family. There are several dialects of Kurdish. Kurmanji Kurdish is spoken by about 12-15 million people, living in Turkey, Iran, Iraq and Syria and is represented by three literary standards and several speech varieties. Some speakers also live in the southern parts of the former Soviet Union, especially in Armenia and Georgia. The following puzzle is from the variety of Kurmanji Kurdish spoken in the former Soviet Union.
