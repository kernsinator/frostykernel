Who We Are

We are Tyler Kerns and Sam Frost. Almost a decade ago, while sharing a dorm room, we discovered some of these online language puzzles and added them to our list of hobbies. Other hobbies at the time included Xbox and pizza. Remembering the fun we had solving these puzzles, as well as the frustration of those that didn't work, we decided to recreate the puzzles here.

Why does this site exist

This page serves as a collection of language puzzles, resources, and a few memes. The main purpose of its creation was to rework some old language puzzles found online that had not been updated or maintained. The puzzles were created by organizations such as theNACLO and its predecessorThe Linguistics Challenge, and others. Each is attributed with the appropriate organization and author. This page only serves to present the completed puzzles with functioning answer-checking, and maybe a more attractive design.
