Bounties

What is a bounty?

We're passionate about languages, but that doesn't necessarily make us experts — there are questions or even entire puzzles that leave us scratching our heads. And in cases where the original webpages don't have answer-checking, we can put forth reasonable guesses but there's no guarantee we actually got it right. In these cases, the question or puzzle will be labeled with an icon like this: !

How do bounties work?

If you know the answer to any question that has a bounty on it, send us a message at [email here] along with an explanation of your reasoning. If we think you nailed it, we'll implement your answer and give you two shout-outs: one here on the bounties page, and one on the puzzle page itself. Plus you get the pride of contributing to language conservation (in our own small way).
