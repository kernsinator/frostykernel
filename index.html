What is FrostyKernel?

This is a place to melt your brain in a linguistic fashion.

The difficulty ratings were provided by the organizations that created the puzzles. You can rest assured that there is something for everyone.

Why does this site exist

This page serves as a collection of language puzzles, resources, and a few memes. The main purpose of its creation was to rework some old language puzzles found online that had not been updated or maintained. The puzzles were created by organizations such as theNACLO and its predecessorThe Linguistics Challenge, among others. Each is attributed with the appropriate organization and author. This site serves to present the completed puzzles with functioning answer-checking, and maybe a more attractive design.
